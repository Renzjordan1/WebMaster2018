Our Story

Based in Louisville, Kentucky, we are an artificial inteligence and robotics company that provides both consumers and producers our open-source technology, in order to increase productivity and convenience. Back in the 2010s, we were fascinated by a new topic, machine-learning A.I, which is A.I. that is constantly learning and improving at doing a specific task, but the A.I. only did one specific complex task. We found 2 major problems with this: it could not do multiple simple tasks like understand language and wasn't as good as humans in many situations. We wanted to eliminate this problem by innovating in the area of artificial general inteligence, inteligence that thinks and acts more like a human. Through strenuous training and development on machine learning, today we are finally able to solve this problem with our awe-inspiring A.I. and robotics that, finally, can talk, think, act like a human, though, not exactly because of our limits of data and compute power for the neural networks, but we believe it will soon become smarter than us. Our code is open-source, allowing the world to maintain control and security of the A.I.

Our Mission

We strive for innovation in the fields of artificial inteligence and robotics. Technology is a powerful tool we must use to our benefit, as it is more productive than a human being at multiple tasks. We are not afraid of technology, which is why we are the inventors of a robot that can detect and treat diseases like cancer. Most would believe that our creation of artificial general inteligence and improved artificial narrow inteligence will cause unemployment, but that is actually a positive aspect. One goal of ours is for society to no longer be forced to do laborous jobs and instead just have with fun our lives. We mustn't be afraid of A.I. being superior to us because they will improve all aspects of our lives like health and happiness.
